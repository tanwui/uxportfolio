Hi, I am Gabriel

I am Sydney-based UX Designer who loves problem-solving and always positions users and clients at the centre of all decision making, with years of experience conceptualising and creating beautiful digital visuals, helping clients to have best experience in using our products.

My background are from Computer Science (Bachelor Degree- Distinction), Digital Design and Digital Photography. I'm also Tridium Niagara 4 Technical Certification Program (TCP) trained and completed freeCodeCamp Responsive Web Design Certification.

Our Clear Comms won Remarkable Tech Design-athon 2020's Challenge #3 Inclusive eSport. Clear Comms is a communication tool that allows game players to record or download phrases for a customisable communication wheel.

And with Code.Sydney team, I built ZONED - an app that can potentially provide more granular data insights to the local neighbourhood at #StayHome Hackathon 2020 Sydney (Note: ZONED prototype is inactive as the API Calls have been removed).
